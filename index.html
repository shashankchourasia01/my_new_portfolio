Project 3

AI Mock Interview Platform

I built an AI Mock Interview Platform that helps users practice and prepare for real interviews in a smart and interactive way. Users can sign up or log in via Clerk to access their dashboard, where they create a mock interview by entering their job role, job description, and years of experience. Based on these details, Gemini AI generates five personalized interview questions. The platform then allows users to switch on their camera and microphone for a real-time interview experience, where they can record their answers one by one. All responses are securely stored in the backend using Drizzle ORM with PostgreSQL, making it easy to track progress. After completion, AI provides feedback, ratings, and correct answers, helping users improve their skills. Users can also revisit their past interviews from the dashboard and directly jump into any specific part of the session. The platform is built with Next.js, Shadcn/UI, Tailwind CSS, Clerk, Drizzle ORM, PostgreSQL, and Gemini AI, ensuring a modern, scalable, and seamless user experience.
